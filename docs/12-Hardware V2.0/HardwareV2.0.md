## Overview

In this section I will discuss what changes I would make to my hardware design if I had the opportunity to redesign my schematic/PCB.

## Changes

The first change I would make would be to connect the VDD of my SNAP and PIC to separate lines because the VDD of my SNAP was shorting my PIC and that was causing me issues for a while. The next change I would make would be to add a way to bring in external power to my microcontroller because after my voltage regulator stopped working I had to use a protoboard for a new voltage regulator and jump it into the pads of my pcb but if I had the foresight off adding a jumper to one of my VDD pins I could have save myself hours of unnecessary work. The final change I would have made would be to use larger footprints for my parts, I had plenty of leftover room on my board and using larger footprints would have made it less tedious to hand solder and could have avoided a lot of bad connections due to bad contact with my pads.
